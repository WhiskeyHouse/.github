# Whiskey House of Kentucky

Welcome to the official GitHub organization for Whiskey House of Kentucky (WHK).
We are dedicated to advancing the craft of whiskey through technology, innovation, and tradition. This organization houses our software projects, tools, and documentation that support operations, manufacturing, and customer engagement.

⸻

🌟 About Us

Whiskey House of Kentucky is a state-of-the-art whiskey distillery and innovation hub located in Bardstown, Kentucky. We combine traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge digital technologies to create high-quality whiskey and build digital ecosystems that empower our operations, customers, and partners.

⸻

📂 Repository Structure

Repositories in this organization typically fall into these categories:
	•	Backend Services: APIs and microservices powering our production and customer platforms.
	•	Frontend Applications: Web and mobile interfaces for customer portals, operations dashboards, and internal tools.
	•	Data & Analytics: Data pipelines, models, and integrations for visibility, governance, and decision-making.
	•	Infrastructure & DevOps: Deployment, monitoring, and cloud-native resources supporting scalability and reliability.
	•	Documentation: Internal and customer-facing docs, guides, and standards.
